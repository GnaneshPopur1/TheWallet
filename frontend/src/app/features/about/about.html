About TheWallet

TheWallet is a student-focused personal finance app designed to track accounts, spending, subscriptions, budgets, academic costs, dining dollars, and roommate splits from one unified dashboard.

Tech Stack

Built exclusively for students and young adults to manage their financial life in one place, TheWallet uses a modern, robust tech stack to ensure security and performance.

  • Frontend: Angular 21, SCSS, Chart.js
  • Backend: Node.js, Express 5
  • Database: Prisma ORM with SQLite
  • Security: JWT authentication, bcrypt hashing, Helmet, and AES-256-GCM encryption for bank connection tokens.
